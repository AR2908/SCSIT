About the School

The School of Computer Science & IT offers postgraduate programmes in Computer Science, Information Technology and related disciplines.

The objective is to prepare students for careers in software development, research, innovation and entrepreneurship in the field of computing.

Teaching is supported by well-equipped labs, experienced faculty members and collaborations with industry and research organizations.
